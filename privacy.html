🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Kid Learn is designed for children to practice basic reading and math skills in a safe and simple learning environment. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring a secure experience for children and parents.

Information We Collect

Kid Learn does not require users to create accounts and does not collect personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, or contact details.

We may collect limited, non-personal usage data (such as screen interactions or feature usage) to improve app performance and learning experience.

How We Use Information

Any collected usage data is used only to:
• Improve app performance
• Fix bugs and technical issues
• Enhance learning features and user experience

Children’s Privacy

Kid Learn is intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If any data is collected unintentionally, it is used only for app functionality and is not shared or sold.

Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services (such as analytics or hosting providers) to operate and improve performance. These services may collect limited anonymous usage data under their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information and ensure it is handled securely. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.
