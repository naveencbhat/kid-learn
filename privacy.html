🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Kid Learn is a learning app designed for children to practice basic reading and math skills in a safe and simple environment. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting user data.

Information We Collect

When you use Kid Learn, we may collect the following information:

• Email address (used for login and account access)
• Authentication data (secure login credentials handled through our system)
• Basic usage data (such as app interactions for improving performance)

How We Use Information

We use collected information only to:
• Provide secure login and account access
• Improve app performance and learning experience
• Fix bugs and enhance features

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. We do not share personal information with third parties except for essential services (such as authentication or hosting).

Children’s Privacy

Kid Learn is intended for children under parental guidance. We only collect the minimum required data (email for login) and do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information.

Future Updates

As we improve Kid Learn and add new features, this Privacy Policy may be updated. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we continuously work to improve security.
